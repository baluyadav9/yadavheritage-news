Sadar Utsav 2026 — 21 October 2026

The iconic Dunnapothula Pandaga (Buffalo Carnival) — now an official Telangana State Festival — promises the grandest edition yet. Preparations are already underway at Narayanguda YMCA, Saidabad, Ameerpet, Khairatabad, Shaikpet, and all 33 districts of Telangana.
